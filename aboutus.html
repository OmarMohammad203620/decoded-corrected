About Us

Decoded is one of the first authentic NFT-driven fashion brands for hoodies in Europe. Each hoodie that we sell has a V-code that could be scanned which will prove the patent and uniqueness of the garment. In case the garment is lost, damaged or sold to somebody else the V-code could be scanned and "Decoded" will send a new piece to the first and only owner in the order he/she to have a new authentic piece, "Decoded" is a brand that challenges the collaboration between art and luxury fashion in a new way making the art wearable and one of a kind.

What we do?

Decoded works with top NFT artists and with each order you will receive a part from their world. However, in order to preserve the authenticity and uniqueness of each piece, you will be only able to see what you got once you have your delivery.

Vision

In a world where we all strive to be unique, we believe that we can do that with only one authentic piece in our wardrobe.
